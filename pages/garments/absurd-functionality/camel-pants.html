How Purchasing Works

Purchasing this digital garment allows you to 'swap' out your clothing for this garment on any given photo you'd like once. This garment will then also automatically be credited to your Silkswap wardrobe for unlimted swaps as soon as our beta opens to the public. If you already have access to the closed beta this garment will be added to your SilkSwap wardrobe immediately.

How Swaps Work

After you are directed to our secure 3rd party billing system and your payment is successful you will be redirected to our website where you can upload a photo to be swapped. Swaps can take between 2 and 6 hours or more during peak times so please be patient.
